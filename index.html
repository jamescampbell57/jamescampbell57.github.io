About

I am an undergrad at Cornell University graduating in December of 2023.

My goal is to make frontier AI systems more interpretable, honest, and controllable.

To that end, I have been a primary contributor to papers such as Representation Engineering and Localizing Lying in Llama. Previously, I have also done work on semantic representations in the brain and LLM robustness.
